CRICKET

Indus Electrical & Electronics Engineering Students’ Association organized its Annual Sports event of Gully Cricket on 9th February’17. More than 50 teams from various Departments of the University participated. Each team consisted of 5 players and each innings was of 5 overs. Mechanical-Automobile team, with the highest score, won the tournament. Alongside cricket, students also participated in other games, namely Tug of War and Slow Cycling. The entire event gained momentum with the active participation of both students & faculty.

BASKETBALL

Gujarat Basketball Team has won their first ever National Medal in All India Basketball Tournament held in Goa from 28th August 2018 to 1st September 2018. They were the runners up in All India West Zone. Sanjay Patel (IU1541060030), student of 7th semester, Electrical Engineering Department was one of the team member of this Gujarat Basketball Team.
